The Best Movies According to Hartmut

My top 3 movies of all-time.

KungFu Panda

is a cherished animated film series that follows the journey of Po, a panda who aspires to become a kung fu master. The films blend martial arts action, humor, and heartwarming moments, creating an enduring appeal for both children and adults. At its core, the franchise inspires viewers with its message of self-discovery and believing in one's inner strength.

Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles" (TMNT)

It is a popular animated series that revolves around four anthropomorphic turtles named Leonardo, Donatello, Michelangelo, and Raphael, who are trained in martial arts by their rat sensei, Splinter. They battle various villains, including their arch-nemesis, the Shredder, while living in the sewers of New York City. TMNT has been adapted into numerous animated series and films, captivating audiences with its action-packed adventures, humor, and themes of teamwork and camaraderie..
